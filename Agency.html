About Us

Behind the Brand

Established in 2005 out of a passion for travel and hospitality, we have grown mindfully and organically, opening offices in key markets and working with clients that have a compelling story to tell. Today, we are a global team of connectors, travelers and storytellers, all with a passion for creating and promoting brands that we love. We are proud to be a recognized and admired agency within the travel and lifestyle space, with a portfolio of clients throughout North America, Europe, the Caribbean, Asia, Australia and beyond.

Longevity Is At Our Core

Relationships and retention are the backbone of J/PR, both with clients and our team. Our team takes pride in building genuine relationships with each other, our clients, the media and brands. At J/PR, we have over 35 clients that have partnered with us for 5+ years and 19 clients that have partnered with us for over 10 years. We are incredibly committed to our team and our talent, and we are honored to have many tenured staff who have worked at J/PR for over 5 and 10 years, with 43 team members who started as interns. This is attributed to our commitment to mentorship and learning opportunities, as well as a collective passion, culture and vision for success.

Storytelling is the foundation of modern communication. It allows brands to express their values, mission, and unique perspective in a way that resonates with their audience. By creating compelling narratives, businesses can inspire trust, build emotional connections, and establish long-term loyalty. A great story doesn’t just inform—it engages, entertains, and makes your brand unforgettable.

Public relations focuses on building a positive image and reputation for brands. Through strategic communication, PR helps companies strengthen relationships with customers, stakeholders, and the media. Effective PR not only promotes products but also creates credibility and trust within the community. In today’s competitive market, a solid PR strategy can be the difference between being noticed or overlooked.

Social media is more than just posting—it’s about engagement, storytelling, and creating communities. It provides a space where brands can interact directly with their audience, share their voice, and receive feedback instantly. With the right strategy, social media increases visibility, drives conversations, and converts followers into loyal customers. Every post has the power to shape your brand identity in the digital world.

Paid social strategy ensures that your content reaches the right audience at the right time. By leveraging data-driven campaigns, brands can target specific demographics and interests. Paid promotions expand visibility beyond organic reach, helping businesses stand out in crowded feeds. With careful planning, paid social can drive traffic, generate leads, and boost overall brand awareness effectively.

Brand partnerships bring together businesses with complementary values and audiences. These collaborations open opportunities for growth, creativity, and increased market reach. A successful partnership creates mutual benefits, where both brands amplify each other’s strengths. By working together, companies can achieve greater visibility and make a bigger impact.

Content creation transforms ideas into meaningful experiences for audiences. From visuals to written copy, content shapes the way people perceive a brand. Creative and high-quality content captures attention, educates, and inspires engagement. It’s not just about what you say—it’s about how you say it and how it connects with your audience.

Influencer marketing allows brands to collaborate with trusted voices who already have engaged audiences. Influencers bring authenticity and credibility, making marketing feel natural and relatable. This strategy can amplify brand visibility, increase conversions, and build meaningful customer relationships. When done right, influencer partnerships can make a lasting impact on consumer perception.

Crisis communication is about managing challenges with clarity and confidence. In difficult times, how a brand responds can make or break its reputation. Transparent, timely, and strategic messaging helps maintain trust and reduce uncertainty. Effective crisis management not only resolves issues but also demonstrates resilience and responsibility.
